Beyonce

Timeline of Life and Career

In 1981, Beyonce Knowles was born in Houston, Texas on September 5th by her mother Tina Knowles-Lawson and father, Matthew Knowles. By the age of 13, Beyonce had already been recognized for her talent in music. As a child, she would sing in the choir and perform in shows for large audiences. Throughout the years, Beyonce has grown as an artist creating some notable ablums such as "Sasha Fierce" and "Formation".
